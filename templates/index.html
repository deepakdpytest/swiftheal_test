F.A.Q's

Q: What is symptoms analyser?
Answer: Symptom analyser is an AI based tool which asks symptoms from the patients and then diagnose the possible disease on the basis of the symptoms like a doctor.
Q: Is symptom analyser an alternative of a doctor?
Answer: NO, sympotm analyser is not an alternative of a doctor. It works just like an assistant to doctor which will help doctor in diagnosing and treatment process in less amount of time.
Q. How physical meet to a doctor would be conducted?
Answer: If a doctor or a patient feels that there is a need of physical visit for the diagnosis or treatment purpose, then an appoint would be fixed, so that the patient can visit to the doctor on that time only to avoid queue.
