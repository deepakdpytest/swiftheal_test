About Us




Welcome to Torpido !

Torpido is dedicated to providing excellence in healthcare for infants, adolescents, adults as well as old age people in a comprehensive manner. Our objective is to reduce the day to day problems faced by patients and doctors like :-

  • 1. deficient manpower
  • 2. unmanageable patient load
  • 3. long patient wait time

We will diagnose the patient based on the symptoms that he/she inputs and accordingly provide the probable differential diagnosis. It is our hope and intention that you receive high quality care and treatment and we will not stop short of anything but the very best !
