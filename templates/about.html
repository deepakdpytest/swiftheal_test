About Us




Welcome to TORPEDO !

Stool is an outcome of biotransformation of various tissues in the body. Ayurveda explains the features of stool in relation to health and morbidity. The reason for neglecting Ayurveda pareeksha may be a deficiency in understanding the features, absence of any validity and reliability checks, etc. One among the least considered is examination of stool, hence current study brings forth mobile application that helps in assessing the health and morbidity through the data that is developed and categorized. Observations mentioned in Charaka samhita regarding the morbidity and health based on stool has been collected by the authors with references. The data was categorized under several domains such as advice or treatment, disease or condition, causes, etc. Duplications on diseases and features of stool were removed from the data base. Content and construct validity was assessed with experts. A total of 1070 references were studied, the data base is capable of identifying 221 disease or morbid conditions, through 171 attributes of feces. TORPEDO 1 comprises of analysis based on Charaka and description of normal stool data base collected from healthy individuals. Further updates will be added as per need. TORPEDO 1 will be a handy tool to understand morbidity and or health based on observations derived from stool on day to day basis as per Ayurveda. The analysis and interpretation along with advice will be reflected in the report.
